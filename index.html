More than a player, she's a symbol of power:Mary Kom

Six-time World Champion and Olympic bronze medalist (2012).

First Indian Woman Boxer

First Indian woman boxer to qualify for the Olympics.

Hailing from Manipur

She is from a small village called Manipur in Northeast India, where she began her boxing journey at the age of 6.

Mother and Athlete:

Mary Kom is a mother of three children, demonstrating that one can balance a successful sports career with family life.
